Goals in this website

Purpose

The overall computer programming professions develop in the current age, but yet, the need of programmers from many companies is still relevant. That's why it is essential to have courses on specific spheres of programming for public needs, whether creating artificial intelligence for cars, or makiing astronomical calculations on other planets, or creating new operating systems. In this website the main focus here is to teach website designs, one of the most important parts of product representations on the internet. It has many needs from various fields: representing online shops, new products, forums on specific questions, even social media such as YouTube. Without good website designs, any product, whether good or bad, wouldn't gain much attention in the long run.

Audience

Everyone, who is capable on understanding the basic consepts of programming (such as variables, condition statements, loops and others) are allowed to learn HTML, CSS and JavaScript features with no problem

CTA (Call To Action)

Users should see the basic HTML tags and their functions that are commonly used in many websites, and can use them for brief summary or as a cheatlist for the future projects that might be usefull
